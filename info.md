## Solar Forecast Card

A Home Assistant Lovelace custom card for displaying solar energy forecast data, with simple setup for Volcast, Solcast, Forecast.Solar, and Open-Meteo Solar Forecast integrations.

### Features

- **7-day forecast bar chart** with colour-coded low/average/high thresholds
- **Hourly popup** — click any day for a detailed hour-by-hour breakdown
- **Live header** — current power output, today's actual generation, next-hour forecast, remaining today
- **Auto-mapping** — select your forecast device and entities are configured automatically
- **Actual generation overlay** — shows today's generation against the forecast bar, with optional multi-array stacked breakdown
- **Export rate display** — shows current energy export rate in the header
- **Solcast P10 support** — optional 10th-percentile secondary forecast values

### Setup

Select your forecast integration device in the card editor to auto-configure all entities, or map them manually. Works with Volcast, Solcast, Forecast.Solar, and Open-Meteo Solar Forecast.
